making ugali

  • 5 cups of water
  • 1 kg of maize flour
  • 2 spoons of margarine
  1. cooking stick
  2. cooking pot

procedure of making ugali

in the pot add 5 litres of water

allow the water boil

in the boiling water add two scoops of margarine

add maize flour to the boiling water and stir the mixture until a thick consistency is attained

keep on watching the ugali cooking for about 5 minutes

remove the pot from the cooking gas

wait for the ugali to cool and slice it to serve
